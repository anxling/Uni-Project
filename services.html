What do we do !

We provide many services for the community and animals in our care which includes all the following services.

    Adoption Day
  • This is run on the first Saturday of the season
  • Come to our shelter and check out our variety of animals to be adopted
  • Learn how to adopt
  • Play with the animals
    Education visits & Centre tours
  • Centre Tours : Monday - Saturday 12pm - 4pm
  • Education visits : Contact us to see when they are available.
  • Bring your kids in to learn about what our shelter does
  • Interact with the animals and learn about them
  • Talk to our volunteers to see how to get involved
