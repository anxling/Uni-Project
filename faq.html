FAQ: Frequently asked questions

How can I go about adopting a animal?

You can inquire via the contact information on our home page to pick up one of our beautiful animals.

Is your shelter ethical?

As a foundation with animals wellbeing in mind, we take great care with each and every animal that is in need- so yes we are ethical.

Do you vaccinate the dogs against parvovirus before they go up for adoption?

Yes, we care deeply about our animals therefore it is essential for all dogs to be vaccinated.

Where do the animals come from?

Many of our animals are brought in by the community as they have been abandoned by their owner or have just lost their way. We also have animals that have come from neglected homes where we have had to interfere to provide better care for the animal.
